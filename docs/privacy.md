# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 19, 2026_

## The Short Version

ScvmBot is not interested in collecting personal data, building user profiles, tracking you, marketing to you, or figuring out who you are.

It exists to do bot stuff, not creep on people.

## What ScvmBot Sees

Because the bot runs on Discord, it may receive basic platform data needed to function, such as:

- user IDs
- server IDs
- channel IDs
- message content used to interact with the bot

## What ScvmBot Does With It

That information is only used to:

- respond to commands
- generate requested content
- keep the bot working
- troubleshoot problems
- deal with abuse or misuse if needed

## What ScvmBot Does Not Do

ScvmBot does not:

- sell your data
- use your data for advertising
- build profiles about you
- intentionally collect more information than it needs to function

## Logging and Retention

ScvmBot is not designed to keep long-term records about you.

Some temporary logs or diagnostic data may exist as part of normal hosting, debugging, or security work, but nothing is kept for marketing, tracking, or resale.

## Third-Party Services

ScvmBot depends on Discord and other infrastructure providers. Those services have their own policies and may collect their own data.

## Changes

This policy may change over time. If it does, the updated version will be posted here.
